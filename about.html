About Ruzik Appliance Repair

A professional technician you can actually trust — honest diagnosis, quality parts, and work done right the first time.

IJ
Your Technician

Isaac-Josiah Olumor

I'm a professional appliance repair technician based in Atlanta, GA, serving Fulton County and the greater Atlanta metro area. I started Ruzik Appliance Repair & Handyman Services to give homeowners something the big chains often can't — a real person who answers the phone, gives you an honest diagnosis, and fixes the actual root cause instead of just the symptom.

I work on dryers, dishwashers, washers, stoves, microwaves, and more — across all major brands including LG, GE, Frigidaire, Whirlpool, Samsung, and Maytag. Every repair starts with a proper diagnostic, not a guess. I use OEM and quality aftermarket parts sourced from trusted suppliers, and I explain exactly what I'm doing and why.

If a repair doesn't make financial sense, I'll tell you that too — no upselling, no unnecessary parts, no wasted service calls.

🔍 Root Cause First

I don't replace parts until I know why they failed. Repeat failures mean the real problem wasn't fixed.

💬 Transparent Communication

You'll know what's wrong, what it costs, and what your options are before any work begins.

⚡ Fast Response

Same-day and next-day service available. I know a broken appliance disrupts your household.
